About World of Warships



Hello, World of warships is a free to play multiplayer online Naval warfare Game created by War Gamming (WG). If you are a new player or a player of world of warships join TAW World of warships division.


Wargaming is steaming up and work hard to develop an environment for competitive World of Warships community within the EU server, also known as World of Warships eSport!


For more info on World of Warships: Counter Strike:GO Home




How to join TAW World of Warships



1) Register yourself first on site: Main Page


2) When the registeration is complete you will be given Team speak3 TAW server name and its password to join.


3) An Email will be SOSsent to you for meetup with a recruiter on Team speak, After the agreement of rules the invite to wows Divison in game will be sent. Accept the invite to join.




Events in World of Warships



World of Warships TAW has its own events called Mandatories. Each member is requested to attend atleast 50% of these events in a month if unable to attend than he must excuse him from that. These Events are a group get togeather for the whole clan 2 times a week. So your attendance is much appreciated and it helps us keep track on our active members.
